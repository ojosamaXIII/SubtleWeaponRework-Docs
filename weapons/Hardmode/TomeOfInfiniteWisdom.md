# Tome of Infinite Wisdom

<img src="../../asset/Tome_of_Infinite_Wisdom.png" width="64">

## Summary

Tornadoes speed up pages; redeploying quickly strengthens the next tornado.

## Original role

The Tome of Infinite Wisdom is a Hardmode magic weapon with both page projectiles and a tornado attack.

It already has a strong identity, but its two attack modes can feel more separate than connected.

## Rework

- While a Tome of Infinite Wisdom tornado is active, page attacks are 25% faster.
- After a tornado disappears, the player has a 4-second redeploy window.
- Redeploying a tornado during this window strengthens the next tornado.
- Redeploy strength can stack up to 3 times.
- Each redeploy stack increases the next tornado's damage and size.

## Notes

This rework connects the Tome's two attack modes.

The tornado sets up faster page attacks, and careful tornado redeployment rewards the player with stronger future tornadoes.
