# Blood Thorn

<img src="../../asset/Blood_Thorn.png" width="64">

## Summary

Can inflict Bleeding; hitting bleeding enemies grants Corpse Pile.

## Original role

The Blood Thorn is a Hardmode magic weapon that summons blood thorns from surfaces.

It has a distinctive casting style, but its combat identity can still feel narrow once enemies move away from ideal terrain.

## Rework

- Blood Thorn hits have a 16% chance to inflict Bleeding for 4 seconds.
- When Blood Thorn hits an enemy that is already Bleeding, the player gains Corpse Pile for 8 seconds.
- While Corpse Pile is active, all player damage is increased by 25%.
- While Corpse Pile is active, mana spending causes life loss based on mana consumed.
- While Corpse Pile is active, non-mana weapon hits also cost a small amount of life.

## Notes

This rework turns the Blood Thorn into a dangerous blood-magic enabler.

Corpse Pile is powerful because it affects all damage, but it asks the player to pay life while taking advantage of the buff.
