# Toxikarp

<img src="../../asset/Toxikarp.png" width="64">

## Summary

Toxic bubbles softly home toward enemies.

## Original role

The Toxikarp is a Hardmode magic weapon obtained through fishing in the Corruption.

Its toxic bubble theme is strong, but the bubbles can feel unreliable because of their slow and unusual movement.

## Rework

- Toxikarp bubbles softly home toward nearby enemies.
- The homing is intentionally gentle rather than aggressive.
- The bubbles keep their original toxic bubble identity while becoming easier to use in real combat.

## Notes

This rework does not try to turn the Toxikarp into a precise tracking weapon.

The goal is to make its bubbles feel less awkward while preserving the strange drifting behavior that makes the weapon recognizable.
