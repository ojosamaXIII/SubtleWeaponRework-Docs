# Flying Knife

<img src="../../asset/Flying_Knife.png" width="64">

## Summary

Repeatedly hitting the same enemy increases damage, up to a cap.

## Original role

The Flying Knife is a Hardmode magic weapon that lets the player control a floating knife projectile.

Its control scheme is unique, but its damage pattern can feel flat once the player is simply holding it on a target.

## Rework

- Repeatedly hitting the same enemy with the Flying Knife increases its damage against that enemy.
- Each consecutive hit grants +10% damage.
- The bonus can stack up to +200% damage.
- The bonus resets if the Flying Knife stops hitting for 4 seconds.
- The bonus also resets when switching to a different target.

## Notes

This rework rewards maintaining pressure on a single target.

The Flying Knife becomes stronger when the player keeps control of the projectile and commits to one enemy instead of drifting between targets.
