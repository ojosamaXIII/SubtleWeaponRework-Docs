# Bladetongue

<img src="../../asset/Bladetongue.png" width="64">

## Summary

Direct blade hits also inflict Ichor.

## Original role

The Bladetongue is a Hardmode sword with an Ichor-themed projectile effect.

Its projectile identity is useful, but the sword's direct blade hits do not feel as strongly tied to that theme.

## Rework

- Direct Bladetongue blade hits also inflict Ichor.
- The Ichor applied by direct blade hits lasts for 7 seconds.

## Notes

This is a small consistency-focused rework.

The Bladetongue already has a strong identity, so the goal is not to reinvent it, but to make the sword itself feel as corrosive as its projectile effect.
