# Staff of Earth

<img src="../../asset/Staff_of_Earth.png" width="64">

## Summary

Costs more mana, but boulders bounce and can hit many more times.

## Original role

The Staff of Earth is a Hardmode magic weapon that fires a heavy boulder projectile.

Its projectile is distinctive, but the weapon can feel clunky because of its heavy movement and limited impact count.

## Rework

- The Staff of Earth's mana cost is increased by 50%.
- Its boulder projectile bounces off tiles instead of disappearing immediately.
- The boulder can hit up to 12 times.
- The boulder uses local hit cooldown to avoid hitting the same enemy too rapidly.

## Notes

This rework makes the Staff of Earth heavier but more rewarding.

The increased mana cost is the tradeoff for turning the boulder into a bouncing, multi-hit terrain weapon.
