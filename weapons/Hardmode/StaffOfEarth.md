# Staff of Earth

<img src="../../asset/Staff_of_Earth.png" width="64">

## Summary

Fires a much larger boulder at the cost of increased mana usage.

## Original role

The Staff of Earth is a Hardmode magic weapon that fires a heavy boulder projectile.

Its projectile is distinctive, but the weapon can feel clunky because its visual impact does not always match the fantasy of hurling a massive chunk of earth.

## Rework

- The Staff of Earth's mana cost is increased by 50%.
- Its boulder projectile is doubled in size.
- The larger boulder keeps the weapon's heavy, high-impact identity while making each cast feel more substantial.

## Notes

This rework makes the Staff of Earth heavier and more visually forceful.

The increased mana cost is the tradeoff for turning each cast into a much larger earth projectile.
