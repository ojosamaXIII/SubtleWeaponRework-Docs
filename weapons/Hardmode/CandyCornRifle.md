# Candy Corn Rifle

<img src="../../asset/Candy_Corn_Rifle.png" width="64">

## Summary

Candy corn bullets bounce off blocks instead of vanishing immediately.

## Original role

The Candy Corn Rifle is a Hardmode ranged weapon from the Pumpkin Moon event.

Its candy corn ammunition is visually distinctive, but the projectiles can feel limited when they vanish on contact with terrain.

## Rework

- Candy corn bullets fired by the Candy Corn Rifle bounce off blocks instead of disappearing immediately.
- Each candy corn bullet can bounce up to 6 times.
- Bounced candy corn bullets lose some speed after each collision.

## Notes

This rework makes the Candy Corn Rifle more chaotic and event-weapon-like.

It becomes better at filling enclosed spaces with bouncing candy corn without directly changing its basic firing role.
