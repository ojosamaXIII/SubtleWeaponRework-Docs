# Poison Staff

<img src="../../asset/Poison_Staff.png" width="64">

## Summary

Hits can spawn poisonous homing shrapnel.

## Original role

The Poison Staff is a Hardmode magic weapon that fires poison projectiles.

Its theme is obvious, but its combat identity can feel like a simple poison projectile weapon.

## Rework

- Poison Staff hits have a 16% chance to spawn poisonous homing shrapnel.
- Up to 3 shrapnel projectiles can be owned by the player at the same time.
- Each shrapnel projectile can hit up to 8 times.
- The shrapnel homes toward enemies and disappears after a limited duration.

## Notes

This rework gives the Poison Staff occasional extra pressure without overwhelming the screen.

The owned-projectile cap keeps the effect controlled while still making the weapon feel more toxic and alive.
