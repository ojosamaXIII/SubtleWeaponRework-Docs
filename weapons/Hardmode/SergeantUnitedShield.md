# Sergeant United Shield

<img src="../../asset/Sergeant_United_Shield.png" width="64">

## Summary

Shield hits build bonus damage while thrown.

## Original role

The Sergeant United Shield is a Hardmode boomerang-like shield weapon.

It already has a strong shield identity, but its thrown behavior can be pushed further toward momentum-based damage.

## Rework

- While the Sergeant United Shield is thrown, its hits build a damage bonus.
- Each previous hit increases later shield hit damage by 10% for that throw.
- The bonus grows as the thrown shield continues hitting enemies.

## Notes

This rework rewards keeping the shield active and landing multiple hits during a throw.

The weapon becomes better when the player uses spacing and enemy groups to let the shield build momentum.
