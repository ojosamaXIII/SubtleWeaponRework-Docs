# Spectre Staff

<img src="../../asset/Spectre_Staff.png" width="64">

## Summary

Spending mana with this staff periodically restores a little life.

## Original role

The Spectre Staff is a Hardmode magic weapon that fires homing spectre projectiles.

Its homing behavior is useful, but the weapon can feel mechanically simple compared with its ghostly theme.

## Rework

- Spending mana with the Spectre Staff builds a mana counter.
- For every 20 mana spent with the staff, the player restores 4 life.
- The healing only occurs if the player is below maximum life.

## Notes

This rework gives the Spectre Staff a light sustain identity.

It does not try to recreate full Spectre armor healing. Instead, it provides small periodic recovery tied directly to mana spent with this weapon.
