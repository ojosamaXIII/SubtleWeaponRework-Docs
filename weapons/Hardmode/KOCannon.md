# K.O. Cannon

<img src="../../asset/KO_Cannon.png" width="64">

## Summary

Greatly increased knockback; confused enemies take double damage from this weapon.

## Original role

The K.O. Cannon is a Hardmode flail dropped during Pirate Invasions.

It has a memorable name and concept, but its role can feel narrow because its main identity is simply hitting hard with knockback.

## Rework

- The K.O. Cannon's knockback is increased to 19.5.
- K.O. Cannon hits inflict Confused on enemies for 4 seconds.
- When the K.O. Cannon hits an enemy that is already Confused, its damage is doubled.

## Notes

This rework pushes the weapon harder into its knockout theme.

It becomes a control-and-punish flail: disorient the target, then land follow-up hits while the enemy is confused.
