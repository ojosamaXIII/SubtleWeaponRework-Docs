# Shadowbeam Staff

<img src="../../asset/Shadowbeam_Staff.png" width="64">

## Summary

Each reflection increases damage, up to a cap.

## Original role

The Shadowbeam Staff is a Hardmode magic weapon that fires a beam which reflects off walls.

Its reflection mechanic is already interesting, but the weapon does not strongly reward clever bounce setups.

## Rework

- Each time a Shadowbeam Staff projectile reflects, its damage increases by 5%.
- The reflection damage bonus can stack up to +50%.

## Notes

This rework rewards using the Shadowbeam Staff in enclosed spaces and aiming for bounce paths.

The weapon becomes more valuable when the player deliberately takes advantage of its ricochet behavior.
