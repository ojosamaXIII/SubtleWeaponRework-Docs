# Pearlwood Bow

<img src="../../asset/Pearlwood_Bow.png" width="64">

## Summary

Stronger arrows call weaker follow-up arrows from off-screen.

## Original role

The Pearlwood Bow is a Hardmode wooden bow crafted from Pearlwood.

Despite being available only after entering Hardmode, it is usually treated as a flavor weapon with very little practical role.

## Rework

- Pearlwood Bow damage is increased.
- When an arrow fired from the Pearlwood Bow hits an enemy, a weaker follow-up arrow is fired from off-screen.
- Each follow-up arrow deals 80% of the previous arrow's damage.
- Follow-up arrows ignore enemy defense.
- The chain stops once the follow-up arrow's damage would fall too low.

## Notes

This rework gives the Pearlwood Bow a distinct Hardmode identity instead of leaving it as a novelty wooden bow.

Its value comes from delayed off-screen follow-up shots, making it feel like a strange Hallow-themed bow rather than a basic stat stick.
