# Marrow

<img src="../../asset/Marrow.png" width="64">

## Summary

Bone arrows inflict Osteoporosis, lowering enemy damage and increasing damage taken.

## Original role

The Marrow is a Hardmode bow with a bone-themed identity.

Its theme is clear, but its combat effect can feel fairly standard compared with more specialized bows.

## Rework

- Arrows fired from the Marrow inflict Osteoporosis for 6 seconds.
- Enemies affected by Osteoporosis deal 10% less damage.
- Enemies affected by Osteoporosis take +4 additional damage from incoming hits.

## Notes

This rework turns the Marrow into a debuff bow.

The effect fits the bone theme by making enemies brittle: weaker offensively and more vulnerable to follow-up hits.
