# Pearlwood Sword

<img src="../../asset/Pearlwood_Sword.png" width="64">

## Summary

Melee hits launch a Magic Missile.

## Original role

The Pearlwood Sword is a Hardmode wooden sword crafted from Pearlwood.

Like the Pearlwood Bow, it arrives late for a basic wooden weapon and usually has almost no practical combat role.

## Rework

- When the Pearlwood Sword hits an enemy with a melee attack, it launches a Magic Missile.
- The Magic Missile deals 45 damage.
- This effect has a short cooldown to prevent excessive missile spam.

## Notes

This rework gives the Pearlwood Sword a magical Hallow-flavored identity.

The sword itself remains simple, but landing melee hits creates a small ranged follow-up that helps justify using a Hardmode wooden weapon.
