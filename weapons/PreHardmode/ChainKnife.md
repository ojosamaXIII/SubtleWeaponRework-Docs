# Chain Knife

<img src="../../asset/Chain_Knife.png" width="64">

## Summary

The Chain Knife is reworked into a bleeding flail that pulls enemies toward the player and becomes more dangerous against wounded targets.

## Original role

The Chain Knife is a rare early flail-style weapon dropped by Cave Bats.

It has a unique appearance and attack style, but its combat identity can feel limited beyond being an unusual early flail.

## Rework

- Chain Knife damage is increased to 20.
- When the Chain Knife hits an enemy, it pulls non-boss enemies slightly toward the player.
- Chain Knife hits inflict Bleeding for 4 seconds.
- When the Chain Knife hits an enemy affected by Bleeding, its damage is increased by 20%.
- When the Chain Knife hits an enemy affected by Bleeding, it also has an additional chance to critically strike.

## Notes

This rework turns the Chain Knife into a close-range control weapon.

It can drag enemies into danger, apply Bleeding, and then punish bleeding targets with stronger follow-up hits.
