# Flintlock Pistol

<img src="../../asset/Flintlock_Pistol.png" width="64">

## Summary

The Flintlock Pistol is reworked into a timing-based pistol that rewards deliberate shots instead of rapid fire.

## Original role

The Flintlock Pistol is an early gun sold by the Arms Dealer.

It is often skipped because players usually need to obtain another gun or bullets before the Arms Dealer appears, meaning the Flintlock Pistol can feel redundant by the time it becomes available.

## Rework

- If the player has not fired the Flintlock Pistol for a short time, the next shot becomes a Quickdraw Shot.
- When Quickdraw is ready, a sound cue plays so the player can recognize the timing without checking the tooltip.
- A fully charged Quickdraw Shot gains increased damage.
- Firing fully charged Quickdraw Shots builds the Quickdraw damage stack.
- Firing before Quickdraw is ready resets the Quickdraw stack.
- Quickdraw Shots have an extra chance to critically strike.
- Quickdraw Shots apply Duel Mark to enemies.
- Flintlock Pistol shots deal additional damage to enemies affected by Duel Mark.

## Notes

This rework is designed to make the Flintlock Pistol feel like a western-style duel weapon.

The goal is not to make it compete with rapid-fire guns directly. Instead, it creates a shoot, wait, draw, and fire rhythm where timing and sound feedback are part of the weapon's identity.
