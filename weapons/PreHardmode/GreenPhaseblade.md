# Green Phaseblade

<img src="../../asset/Green_Phaseblade.png" width="64">

## Summary

The Green Phaseblade is reworked into a poison-focused Phaseblade that spends meteor counters to make its hits poison enemies.

## Original role

The Green Phaseblade is one of the gem-colored Phaseblades.

Its color is distinct, but its original combat behavior is mostly the same as the other Phaseblades.

## Rework

- Green Phaseblade hits build meteor counters.
- The Green Phaseblade can store up to 8 meteor counters.
- Right-click spends 3 meteor counters to activate its color-specific effect.
- While the effect is active, Green Phaseblade hits inflict Poisoned on enemies.
- The weapon effect lasts for 6 seconds.
- Poisoned applied by this effect lasts for 6 seconds.

## Notes

This rework gives the Green Phaseblade a poison identity that fits its color.

It sits between the low-cost Red Phaseblade and the more specialized Purple Phaseblade, giving it a straightforward status role without making it overly complex.
