# Rally

<img src="../../asset/Rally.png" width="64">

## Summary

The Rally is reworked into a precision yoyo that rewards repeatedly hitting the same enemy.

## Original role

The Rally is an early yoyo with a simple attack pattern.

It is useful as an introductory yoyo, but its combat identity can feel plain once the player gets access to stronger options.

## Rework

- Rally hits are tracked separately for each enemy.
- Every third Rally hit on the same enemy deals triple damage.
- The hit counter resets after a short time if the player stops hitting that enemy.

## Notes

This rework keeps the Rally simple, but gives it a clear rhythm.

The weapon rewards staying on target and maintaining contact with the same enemy instead of casually switching targets.
