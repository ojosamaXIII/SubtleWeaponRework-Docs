# White Phaseblade

<img src="../../asset/White_Phaseblade.png" width="64">

## Summary

The White Phaseblade is reworked into a defensive Phaseblade that spends meteor counters to gain a long-lasting defense bonus.

## Original role

The White Phaseblade is one of the gem-colored Phaseblades.

Its original distinction is mainly its color, with little mechanical difference from the rest of the Phaseblade set.

## Rework

- White Phaseblade hits build meteor counters.
- The White Phaseblade can store up to 10 meteor counters.
- Right-click spends 10 meteor counters to activate its color-specific effect.
- The effect grants +8 defense.
- The defense bonus lasts for 40 seconds.

## Notes

This rework makes the White Phaseblade the most defensive member of the Phaseblade set.

It has the highest special cost among the colors, but it rewards sustained combat with a long-lasting defensive payoff.
