# Frost Daggerfish

<img src="../../asset/Frost_Daggerfish.png" width="64">

## Summary

The Frost Daggerfish is reworked into a simple frost-themed throwing weapon that can apply Frostburn on hit.

## Original role

The Frost Daggerfish is a consumable throwing weapon obtained through fishing.

It has a distinctive item concept, but its combat identity is limited because it mostly functions as a basic thrown projectile.

## Rework

- Frost Daggerfish hits inflict Frostburn on enemies for 4 seconds.

## Notes

This is a small, direct rework meant to strengthen the weapon's ice identity.

The Frost Daggerfish remains simple, but now has a clearer role as an early frost-status throwing weapon.
