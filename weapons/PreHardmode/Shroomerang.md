# Shroomerang

<img src="../../asset/Shroomerang.png" width="64">

## Summary

The Shroomerang is reworked into a mushroom-themed boomerang that leaves drifting damaging mushrooms while returning to the player.

## Original role

The Shroomerang is a boomerang obtained from mushroom-themed enemies.

It has a memorable name and theme, but in combat it mostly behaves like a standard boomerang.

## Rework

When the Shroomerang is returning to the player, it periodically drops small mushroom projectiles.

These mushroom projectiles drift for a short time and can damage enemies.

Each mushroom can hit up to 4 times before disappearing.

The mushrooms also disappear after a short duration.

## Notes

This rework gives the Shroomerang a stronger mushroom identity without changing its basic boomerang role.

It rewards positioning and return-path control, since the extra mushrooms appear while the weapon is coming back.
