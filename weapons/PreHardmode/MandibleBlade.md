# Mandible Blade

<img src="../../asset/Mandible_Blade.png" width="64">

## Summary

The Mandible Blade is reworked into an antlion-themed melee weapon with a right-click Mandible Crush attack.

## Original role

The Mandible Blade is a desert-themed melee weapon.

Its appearance is distinctive, but its combat behavior can feel too close to a normal sword despite its antlion mandible design.

## Rework

- Right-clicking with the Mandible Blade performs Mandible Crush.
- Mandible Crush creates two jaw-like slashes in front of the player.
- Mandible Crush inflicts Bleeding for 4 seconds.
- If both jaw slashes hit the same enemy in a short window, the follow-up hit deals reduced damage.
- Mandible Crush has a short cooldown.

## Notes

This rework makes the weapon behave more like a pair of crushing antlion jaws.

The normal attack remains simple, while right-click gives the weapon a distinct identity as a close-range bite-and-crush melee option.
