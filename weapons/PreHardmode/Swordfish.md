# Swordfish

<img src="../../asset/Swordfish.png" width="64">

## Summary

The Swordfish is reworked into a water-focused spear that performs better while the player is underwater.

## Original role

The Swordfish is a spear obtained through fishing.

Its theme strongly suggests an aquatic weapon, but in normal use it does not have much special interaction with water.

## Rework

While the player is underwater, Swordfish damage is increased by 20%.

While the player is underwater, Swordfish attack speed is increased by 50%.

## Notes

This rework makes the Swordfish feel more connected to its fishing and aquatic identity.

It remains a simple spear on land, but becomes noticeably stronger when used in underwater combat.
