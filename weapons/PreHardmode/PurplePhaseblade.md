# Purple Phaseblade

<img src="../../asset/Purple_Phaseblade.png" width="64">

## Summary

The Purple Phaseblade is reworked into a shadowflame-focused Phaseblade that spends meteor counters to apply a stronger magical burn.

## Original role

The Purple Phaseblade is one of the gem-colored Phaseblades.

Like the other Phaseblades, its original difference is mostly visual rather than mechanical.

## Rework

- Purple Phaseblade hits build meteor counters.
- The Purple Phaseblade can store up to 9 meteor counters.
- Right-click spends 6 meteor counters to activate its color-specific effect.
- While the effect is active, Purple Phaseblade hits inflict Shadowflame on enemies.
- The weapon effect lasts for 2 seconds.
- Shadowflame applied by this effect lasts for 4 seconds.

## Notes

This rework makes the Purple Phaseblade a short-window status weapon.

Its effect is stronger than basic fire or poison, but it costs more counters and has a shorter activation window, so it rewards timing the special before committing to hits.
