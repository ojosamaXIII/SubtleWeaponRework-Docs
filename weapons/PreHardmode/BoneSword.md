# Bone Sword

<img src="../../asset/Bone_Sword.png" width="64">

## Summary

The Bone Sword is reworked into a skeletal melee weapon that can fire bone projectiles while swinging.

## Original role

The Bone Sword is a rare melee weapon dropped by Skeletons.

It is thematically strong, but in combat it mostly behaves like a normal sword.

## Rework

- When the Bone Sword is swung, it can fire bone projectiles.
- Each swing can fire up to 2 bone projectiles.
- Each potential bone projectile has a 50% chance to fire.
- Bone projectiles deal reduced Bone Sword damage.
- The projectile volley has a short cooldown to prevent excessive spam.

## Notes

This rework gives the Bone Sword a stronger skeletal identity.

The weapon still works as a melee sword, but now occasionally throws bones forward, making it feel more like a weapon made from restless dungeon bones.
