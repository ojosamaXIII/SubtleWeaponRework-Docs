# Blue Phaseblade

<img src="../../asset/Blue_Phaseblade.png" width="64">

## Summary

The Blue Phaseblade is reworked into a defensive utility Phaseblade that spends meteor counters to gain Wet and brief life regeneration.

## Original role

The Blue Phaseblade is one of the gem-colored Phaseblades.

Like the other Phaseblades, its original identity is mostly visual: it is a colored sword with the same basic role as the rest of the set.

## Rework

- Blue Phaseblade hits build meteor counters.
- The Blue Phaseblade can store up to 6 meteor counters.
- Right-click spends 2 meteor counters to activate its color-specific effect.
- The effect makes the player Wet for 6 seconds.
- The effect also grants brief life regeneration for 6 seconds.
- While the regeneration effect is active, the player recovers 1 life at regular intervals.

## Notes

This rework makes the Blue Phaseblade the sustain-oriented member of the Phaseblade set.

It is not meant to be the strongest damage option among the colors. Instead, it offers a small defensive and recovery tool for players who keep landing hits and spend their counters at the right time.
